IngeniumSTEM

At IngeniumSTEM, our mission is to provide free education related to technology to everyone, especially those in need. Inspired to bridge the gap in STEM education, Kaushik Sannidhi founded IngeniumSTEM to fulfill this goal, a student-run nonprofit dedicated to providing free and equal education to help students be successful in the modern world.

More About Us

At IngeniumSTEM, our main mission is deeply rooted in a passion for revolutionizing education by making it accessible to everyone, especially those in need. Our founder Kaushik Sannidhi initially conceived the idea out of a desire to bridge the gap between the privileged and the underserved in terms of STEM education. Recognizing the transformative power of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, Kaushik envisioned a world where every individual, regardless of background or circumstance, could access high-quality STEM learning opportunities. Driven by this vision, Kaushik embarked on a journey to bring his dream to life. Along the way, he met like-minded individuals who shared his unwavering commitment to democratizing education. Together, they founded IngeniumSTEM, a student-run not-for-profit organization dedicated to providing free and equitable STEM education to learners of all ages. At the heart of our mission is the belief that education should not be a privilege but a fundamental right. We strive to break down barriers and create pathways for every student to explore and excel in STEM subjects. Through innovative programs, interactive workshops, mentorship opportunities, and community-driven initiatives, we aim to inspire curiosity, foster creativity, and empower future innovators. IngeniumSTEM is not just about teaching STEM; it's about igniting passion, nurturing talent, and creating positive change in the world. We are a diverse team of passionate individuals united by a common goal: to revolutionize education and build a brighter future for generations to come. Join us in our mission to make STEM education accessible to all and pave the way for a more inclusive and innovative society.
